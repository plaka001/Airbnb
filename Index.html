¿Is my space suitable for Airbnb?
Guests on Airbnb are interested in all kinds of places. We have listings for tiny houses, cabins, treehouses, and much more. Even a spare room in your own home can be an excellent place to stay.
¿Do I have to share my space all the time?
Not at all: you control the calendar. You can offer hosting services once a year, a few nights per month, or even more frequently.
¿How much should I interact with the guests?
It's up to you! Some hosts prefer to send messages to guests only at key moments, like a brief note during check-in, while others prefer to meet them in person. You'll find a style that works for you and your guests.
¿Do you have any tips for being a good host on Airbnb?
The most important thing is that you understand the basics. Keep your space clean, respond promptly to guests, and provide them with the services they need, such as clean towels. Some hosts like to add a personal touch, such as placing fresh flowers or sharing a list of local places to explore, but none of this is mandatory.
¿What are the fees on Airbnb?
Airbnb usually charges a fixed service fee of 3% of the reservation subtotal when you receive payment. We also charge a fee to guests when they make a reservation. In many areas, Airbnb automatically collects sales and tourism taxes and pays them on your behalf.
